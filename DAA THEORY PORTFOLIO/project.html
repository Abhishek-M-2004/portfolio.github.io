Course Project: Warehouse Management System

We arrived at the need statement from the 'Streets-white paper'. We arrived at the problem space from the document after knowing that there was need for an effecient warehouse management system in the designed smart city.


Problem Definition:

TIn the rapidly evolving landscape of smart cities, efficient warehouse systems are critical to meet the demands of urban living. Smart cities require warehouses that are not only space-efficient but also capable of addressing challenges such as rising consumer demand, route optimization, accurate identification of personnel and vehicles, and budget management. Developing a system that integrates advanced data structures and algorithms will optimize operations, improve resource utilization, reduce costs, and enhance productivity, thereby ensuring seamless warehouse operations in constrained urban environments.
