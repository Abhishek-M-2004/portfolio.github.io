Introduction to the Course

This course focuses on the fundamental principles of designing and analyzing algorithms. It explores efficient data structures and algorithmic techniques to solve a wide range of computational problems. We learn to evaluate the performance of algorithms in terms of time and space complexity, and optimize solutions for efficiency.

Connection of the course with real-world applications

  • Search Engines: Utilize efficient search algorithms (e.g., BFS, DFS) to index and retrieve information quickly.
  • Social Networks: Employ graph algorithms to analyze connections, recommend friends, and detect communities.
  • GPS Navigation: Utilize shortest path algorithms (e.g., Dijkstra's) to find the optimal routes.
  • E-commerce: Implement efficient sorting and searching algorithms for product recommendations and inventory management.
  • Artificial Intelligence: Utilize machine learning algorithms, which often rely on efficient data structures and algorithms for training and prediction.
  • Game Development: Employ pathfinding algorithms (e.g., A*) for character movement and AI decision-making.
